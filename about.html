History

Molinas Construction Company is a family-owned and operated leading provider of construction services and supplies for Federal, State and Local Governments. The company based in Portland, Oregon was founded in 2015 on a shoestring budget and $3,000 loan. Founded by former Army Captain David Molina, Molinas Construction Company has grown out of the family's garage to work on federal projects as a prime and subcontractor. David went into federal contracting shortly after exiting active-duty at Dover Air Force Base in Delaware.

Experience

Over the last 3 years, Molinas Construction has steadily grown to provide services throughout the pacific northwest area. In 2015, Molinas Construction was the successful low bidder for the Mill Creek Levee Vegetation Maintance Phase I job that propelled the company forward. This was the first major public works project by Molinas Construction providing the team project management, supervision, quality assurance, and day-to-day contracting experience. In early 2017, Molinas Construction was awarded and completed base year for the Willamette National Cemetery dirt removal project with our President/Owner operating a 316E CAT to remove and load over 3,000 cubic yards to a fleet of dump trucks. Whether working as a general contractor or as part of a larger team, Molinas Construction fosters a spirit of innovation, cooperation and mutual respect toward a common goal. Known for building lasting relationships with clients, partners, general contractors, and subcontractors, Molinas Construction is dedicated to creating the best possible experience. We take pride in creating safe working conditions, providing quality work, and preserving our environment for future generations.

Since launching, Molinas Construction has performed work for the following federal agencies and general contractors:

  • Department of the Army, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Prime)
  • Department of Veterans Affairs (Prime)
  • U.S. Air Force (Prime)
  • Hanke Constructors (Subcontractor)

Certifications

For federal work, Molinas Construction Company is a U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) CVE Certified Service-Disabled, Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and, fully eligible to compete and perform any VA work.

In Oregon, Molinas Construction is an DBE/MBE/ESB and proud VOSB Certified General Contractor. Certification number 9876.

Leadership

David Molina, President/Owner

David Molina founded Molinas Construction Company in 2015 after serving over a decade in uniform as an Army enlisted soldier and officer. As a Captain in his last active-duty assignment, David proudly served as the S-2/3 OIC (Plans, Training & Operations Officer) at Dover Air Force Base in support of the Families of the Fallen mission culminating over a decade in uniform earning the Army Meritorious Service Medal, Army Commendation Medal, and Lt. Rowan Award. Throughout a decade as a citizen-soldier (2000-2014), David served as a Private in the 364th Civil Affairs Brigade, as a 2nd Lt and Summary Court Martial Officer at the Joint Personal Effects Depot-Casualty Mortuary Affairs Operations Center, and Aide-de-Camp to the Assistant Division Commander-Operations at the 104th Division (IT).

As a forward-facing leader, David was the project manager on Molinas Construction first public works project, operating STIHL chainsaws, TORO stumpgrinders and directing the team towards mission completion. Not afraid to wear his muck boots, David operated a 316E CAT to move over 3,000 cubic yards in environmentally sensitive operating conditions. In its brief, young history, Molinas Construction has grown from a garage-based small landscaping company to a federal contractor headquartered in downtown Portland trained through trial by fire. As Molinas President, David oversees all aspects of business from business development, human resources, field operations, equipment and asset management, strategic planning and growth initiatives.

David Molina will never ask anyone on his team to do anything he isn't willing to do first.

Outside of Molinas Construction, David is an active community member and mentor to many startups, future entrepreneurs, and veterans transitioning off active-duty. David serves on the Board of Director of LatinoBuilt, and his local chapter of the American Society of Professional Estimators. Previously, he founded Operation Code, a 501c(3) nonprofit serving military veterans and military dependents across 19-time zones to learn to code and get into tech. He previously served on the Oregon Community Foundation Latino philanthropy advisory council, Oregon State University Board for Diversity and Inclusion, and the Governor's Commission on Hispanic Affairs representing small business interests.
